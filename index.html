Project 02

Noetix robotics product family / 松延动力

At Noetix Robotics, I worked on an upper-body industrial design language shared across multiple bionic and interactive robot products. This section includes Xiao Nuo as an interactive humanoid communication product and Hobbs 3.0 as a producible bionic service robot, both built from the torso and neck design system I developed.

Company Background
Scope Upper-body product identity, torso and neck surfacing, screen integration
Timeline Feb. 2025 - Apr. 2025

06

Xiao Nuo, interactive humanoid communication product

Xiao Nuo is an interactive product used for face-to-face communication, public demonstration, and emotionally expressive interaction. These images combine surfaced CAD, prototype photography, and public event scenes to show how the upper-body design supports friendliness, readability, and presence.

Design focus

The key challenge in Xiao Nuo was balancing product intimacy with robotic credibility. The head, neck, chest screen, and shoulder transitions all had to work together so the robot could feel approachable while still reading as a coherent technology product.

07

Hobbs 3.0, producible bionic service robot

Hobbs 3.0 extends the same torso and neck design language into another product line. As you described it, Noetix Robotics Hobbs 3.0 is the world's first producible bionic service robot, featuring built-in cameras for eyes. It was displayed at the Appliance and Electronics World Expo in Shanghai, China.

Shared design language

Hobbs 3.0 carries the same torso and neck system I designed. Including it here shows how one upper-body design language can scale across multiple interactive robot products with different faces, scenarios, and public roles.

Company Background

Short company notes for the two robotics projects featured on this website.

Project 01 Company

DeepCybo

DeepCybo, officially DeepCybo (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd., is an embodied intelligence company jointly incubated in 2025 by Beijing Zhongguancun Academy and the Zhongguancun Academy of Artificial Intelligence. The company focuses on general embodied intelligence driven by first-person human data.

Project 02 Company

Noetix Robotics

Noetix Robotics, officially Songyan Dynamics (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd., was established on September 15, 2023 in Beijing. The company focuses on general AI embodiments, robotics biomimicry, and embodied operating systems, with products spanning the N series, general humanoids, and Hobbs biomimetic robots.
