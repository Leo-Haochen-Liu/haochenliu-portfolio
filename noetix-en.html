Project 02

Noetix Xiao Nuo

Upper-body product language, face-and-screen integration, and public interaction scenarios.

At Noetix Robotics, I participated in a reusable upper-body industrial design language for biomimetic and interactive robot products. This work includes Xiao Nuo, designed for face-to-face communication scenarios, and extends to product lines such as the biomimetic service robot Hobbs 3.0.

My Role

Upper-body product identity, torso and neck surfacing, and integrated face-and-screen expression.

Project Extension

The same design language was extended into product lines including Hobbs 3.0.

Company Background

Songyan Dynamics (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd.

Noetix Robotics, officially Songyan Dynamics (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd., was founded on September 15, 2023 and is headquartered in Beijing. The company develops general AI embodiments, biomimetic robotics, and embodied operating systems, with product lines including the N series, general humanoid robots, and Hobbs biomimetic robots.
